Exploring World Happiness

Welcome to our interactive slideshow visual on world happiness! In this journey, we'll explore the happiness levels of different countries around the globe and examine various aspects that contribute to overall well-being.

Happiness is a complex and multidimensional concept. It encompasses not only subjective feelings of joy and satisfaction but also objective factors like health, economic stability, social support, and more.

Through interactive charts and insightful data analysis, we'll delve into the factors that influence happiness and uncover fascinating trends and patterns across different regions and years. Get ready to embark on this exciting adventure of understanding global happiness!
